Ciao!

I'm Antonello, I was born and raised in Rome, Italy, now I'm living and working in Rotterdam, Netherlands.
I'm a musician, I play in a folk rock band and do some other related things, and a software engineer, I work for a company in Rotterdam and do some other related things.
Below you'll fine some of the projects I've been working on, and here some travel photos.

Music

Johnny Bemolle's

It's a folk/rock and chamber music project: we found some songs in an old suite case, all of them signed by Johnny Bemolle, a mysterious vagabond musician, and each of them talking about a different place. We (self) produced:

  • a record ("Jb")
  • a crowdfunding campaign
  • a music video
  • a theatre show
Find out more on johnnybemolle.com and Facebook, listen on Spotify, or buy Jb on Bandcamp.

Theatre music

I compose and record music for an amateur acting company (L'Eclettica) theatre shows. On Jamendo you can listen, download and license this music, and use it for your own projects:

www.jamendo.com/artist/455477/antodippo

Software

I'm a software engineer, and I've been working for different companies and agencies, you can see my CV on LinkedIn and some of my code on GitHub. Sometimes I also speak at conferences, for example this talk was about Composer. I have also some side projects:

CC music search

It's a search engine for Creative Commons music, written in PHP on Symfony framework. It fetches music from Jamendo, Soundcloud and other services, by genres, instruments or other keywords.
You can try it on http://ccmusicsearch.com, and see source code on GitHub.

Mappics

A map based travel photos gallery, with automatic place and weather description of the very moment the photos are taken. You can try it on http://pics.antodippo.com, and see source code on GitHub.
